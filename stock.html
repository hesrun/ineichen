FAQ

How can I apply for participation?

You have to go through a simple verification process:

  1. Verify and complete your profile
  2. Fill out a form to receive an NDA from us
  3. Sign in and send us back the signed NDA

You will be verified as soon as our team checks the document. It takes 1-2 days max.

How long will it take to announce the winners?

We will try our best to inform you as soon as possible, the final winners will be announced by August 30.

How do I know that Ineichen Zurich AG is a good investment?

After you have been approved for participation you will be able to access our detailed business plan describing all results achieved by our company and proving exactly why Ineichen Zurich AG would be a great addition to your investment portfolio.

Why have you selected this format of offering?

As a private business we are bounded by certain legal obligations in terms of public our offering would be, yet we believe that our potential investors can not be reached through venture funds or limited to publicly known investors. We wanted to make this opportunity as accessible as possible, therefore, we came up with the current solution.

What rights do I have as a new co-owner?
  • To participate in the shareholders' meetings
  • To influence the important decisions of the company
  • To resell your rightfully owned shares for profit
  • To visit the Zurich HQ as your second home
  • To enjoy the beauty of the luxury watches every day
How will the winners be selected if the bids are equal?

Your bid amount plays an important role for sure. However, we consider multiple factors. Our goal is to find the new co-owners, who will be actively involved in the business, not just silent investors! Your activity and enthusiasm as well as professional background will be also considered as important factors for decision making. Please do not hesitate to contact us and talk through the important issues!

What happens next if I win the auction?

You will be notified and contacted by our legal team to reconfirm your victory. As soon as the payment is received, and the shareholders’ agreement is signed, the registration process will be set up. Since Ineichen Auctioneers is registered on the Daura platform, the only thing we will need to complete the transfer of the shares is to simply create a verified account on the platform!
